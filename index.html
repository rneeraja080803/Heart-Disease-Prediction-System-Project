Understanding Risk Levels

🟢

Low Risk — No Disease

Clinical parameters are within healthy ranges. Normal BP, low cholesterol, high exercise heart rate, and no blocked vessels indicate good cardiovascular health.

🟡

Medium Risk — Borderline

Some parameters are slightly elevated. Borderline BP, moderate cholesterol, or mild ST depression suggest watchfulness. Lifestyle changes and regular monitoring are recommended.

🔴

High Risk — Disease Detected

Multiple critical indicators present. Asymptomatic chest pain, high BP, low max heart rate, exercise angina, and reversible thalassemia defect strongly indicate coronary artery disease.

📊

How the 3 Models Vote

Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and SVM each predict independently. The consensus panel shows the dominant risk level. All three agreeing gives the highest confidence.
